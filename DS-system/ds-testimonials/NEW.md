# ds-testimonials

## What this component is

The testimonials section on the self-paced courses page.

## Where it is used

Fourth content section in `main#main`.

## How it is used

It provides social proof through a slider of learner quotes and navigation controls.

## Structure

`section.ds-wrapper.ds-testimonials.gold` containing a `div.slider` with a heading, arrow buttons, and a `ul.testimonials-primary`.

## Variants
